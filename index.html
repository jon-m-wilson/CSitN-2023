About

The North of England is becoming a hotspot for the study of cluster algebras. This one-day conference aims to gather the growing community to discuss the latest developments in cluster theory, and chart out new directions for further research.

Date of conference: Wednesday 28th June, 2023

Location: University of Central Lancashire, Preston.

Registration : Registration is now open! (The registration form closes on Wednesday 21st June, but please email the organiser directly if you wish to register after this date).
